::Project_02_Create IAM Service(Identity and Access Management) User on AWS::

I want to Create IAM User by using AWS Management Console(Inside Root user)

Step 1: first go to "Google Chrome" and "Search on Google Chrome", write "AWS Management Console" and after click on that "Enter" button.

Step 2: After click on that first link "AWS Management Console" and after click on that "Enter"

Step 3: After click on that "Sign in Console"

Step 4: After you give "gmail id" inside "root user"

Step 5: After you give "Password" inside "root user"

Step 6: After you can see in below "AWS CONSOLE HOME"

click on that "IAM Service". and after you can see also "IAM Console dashboard".

Step 8: Go to the "left side corner" click on that "users", and after click on that "Create User" inside "IAM Service.

Step 9: Inside Specify user details, you can give "User name:User1", and you can select the checkbox "Provide user access to the AWS Management Console - optional" (Note: but this is optional for using checkbox), after you can select the radiobutton "Custom password" and give custom password: "Abd@1234" and after select the checkbox "User must create a new password at next sign-in Recommended" and after click on that "Next" button.

Step 10: Inside "Set permissions" there is a prompt called "Permission options", there are three options, but you can select one radio-button box "Add user to group" and click on that "Next" button.

Step 11: Inside "Review and Create", keep in default everything, and after click on that "Create User", after you can see message on dashboard "User Created Sucessfully". you can copy the "Console sign-in URL" or download the "Download .csv file", and store in any "folders".

Step 12: Inside "Review and Create", you can copy the "Console sign-in URL" and Paste that "URL" in another "Google Chrome Browser" and after click on that "Enter" button. It redirect you to see the "AWS IAM User Sign-in page". after Inside AWS dashboard having "Sign in as IAM user" Here you have to provide "IAM user name: User1 and also provide "Password":Abd@1234 to sign-in into the IAM users Account and after click on that "Sign-in".

Step 13: Inside AWS dashboard, In IAM user1, you can give old password: Abd@1234, after you can give "New Password" and Retype new password: Abd@12345 and after click on that "Confirm password change".

Step 14: In below you can see "AWS CONSOLE HOME IAM user1" Created Sucessfully.
